Exercise demo videos

Add a MuscleWiki API key to show real looping exercise demos inside each exercise, instead of leaving the app. The video appears at the top of an exercise when you open it.

Get a key at api.musclewiki.com/register. Direct video access needs the TESTING tier ($10/mo) — the free BASIC tier is playground-only and won't stream video. Without a key, the app still works using the written form cues and the demo link. Your key is stored only on this device.
Back up your data. Your weekly plans, training logs, workout history, food log and weight history are stored only on this phone — not in the cloud. If you clear Safari's data or change phones, they're gone. Export below and keep the text somewhere safe (a note, or email it to yourself).
